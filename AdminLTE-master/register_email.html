Set up Hutch

Start using Hutch by selecting your user settings.  Click on the settings icon in the top right. Click "Settings"

Under settings, you can select your units, breed schedule chain of events, pedigree generations, and enter your Rabbitry information and logo to appear on Pedigrees.

Add Breeders

Once you have your basic settings done, you can add Breeder Rabbits. Go to the menu (on mobile, tap the menu icon on the left green bar) and click/tap "Breeders"

Start by adding a new breeder, and once you have a few of your bucks and does in the system, you can add current litters, schedule breeds and other tasks.

Dashboard

The Dashboard is the home page for Hutch.  From here, you can add a breed chain (Breed) to a buck/doe, report a birth in an existing chain (Birth), enter weights for litters (Weigh), and record a butcher event (Butcher). Below these quick tasks is the schedule, where your tasks are displayed, as well as a weekly outlook for upcoming tasks.
